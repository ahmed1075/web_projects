WHAT WE DO

SchoolProvement provides a mobile/web app that allows school communities to improve attendance, communication, feedback, and habits for their schools.
We work in countries and regions where teacher absenteeism and school culture are issues that school districts and communities want to improve.

WHO WE ARE

An organization (applying to be a 501(c)(3)) consisting of people who are passionate about student success across the world.
As Returned Peace Corps Volunteers and entrepreneurs, we care about creating solutions to difficult development challenges for all communities.

WE IMPROVE SCHOOLS

  • Better Teacher Attendance

  • through monitoring & verification of teachers' absences.

  • Direct & Easy School Communications

  • with updates sent by Principals and Teachers directly to users back home.

  • Highlighting Best Schools & Teachers

  • by allowing anonymous feedback & ratings for teachers and schools.

  • Encouraging Positive School Habits

  • with habit "missions" like eating breakfast before school and greeting students at the door with a smile.
OUR HISTORY

After seeing the impacts of teacher absenteeism and poor school culture in the Federated States of Micronesia as Peace Corps Volunteers, SchoolProvement (formerly Raise Your Text) was founded and piloted in Pohnpei, FSM in 2018.
Now the organization seeks to expand to other countries where teacher absenteeism is a serious issue.
